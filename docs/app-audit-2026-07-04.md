**Scope:** `apps/web` (Dev 2's domain) — the entire frontend, not a diff. Backend (`apps/api`) and other devs' domains are out of scope.
**Method:** 5 parallel review agents, each covering a subsystem (player/lesson, auth/middleware/shell, dashboard/upload/library/settings, landing/marketing, test coverage), run after S2-03 (Onboarding Assessment Flow) shipped. 37 distinct findings, then cross-referenced against `docs/dev2-sprint-tracker.md` to separate real bugs from already-planned future-sprint work.
